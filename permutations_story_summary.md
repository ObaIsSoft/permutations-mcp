**Version:** 0.0.8 | **Last Updated:** 2026-03-14

## The Origin Story

### The Problem
Every AI-generated website looks identical

- Inter font + blue-purple gradients (the 'SaaS starter pack')
- Rounded-xl cards in a 3-column grid
- Generic hero section with 'Trusted by' logos
- Parallax scroll that induces motion sickness
- Same Tailwind UI components copied endlessly

LLMs default to 'safe' patterns because they have no constraints forcing creativity. Without guardrails, AI converges on the statistical average of training data—resulting in the same mediocrity everywhere.
